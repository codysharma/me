Percent Taking the SAT and Mean Score by State - 2023

  • Category: Data Visualization
  • Project URL: Link to Tableau

Description

I found a dataset showing both the mean score by state and the percent of students taking the SAT. It seemed like there was a pattern between the two, and indeed on initial glance, it seems to be a correlation between higher average scores and smaller percentage of students taking the test. I chose to overlap the results onto geographic data to allow me to show all pieces of information.
